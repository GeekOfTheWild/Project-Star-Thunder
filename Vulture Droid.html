Vulture Droid

The Vulture Droid is the Seperatists Main fighter for most of the Clone Wars. It is equipped with twin blaster cannons which is a bit on the light side but is also equipped with 2 energy torpedo launchers and buzz droid launchers

Type: Droid Starfighter

Manufacturer: Xi Charrian

Model: Vulture-class

Speed: 1,180 kph (733 mph)

Length: 3.5m (11ft 6 in)

Hyperdrive: None

Weapons: 2 blaster cannons, 2 energy torpedo launchers and buzz droid launchers
