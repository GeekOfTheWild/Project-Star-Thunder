V-19 Torrent Starfighter

The V-19 Torrent Starfighter is a powerful-ish Republic fighter. It is equipped with 2 laser cannons and concussion 6 missiles. It is best used in dogfights engaging enemy fighters and attacking bombers.

Type: Starfighter

Manufacturer: Slayn & Korpil

Model: V-19 Torrent Starfighter

Speed: 1,200 kph (745 mph)

Length: 6m (19ft 8 in)

Hyperdrive: Class 1 (early models used an externable hyperdrive ring)

Weapons: 2 laser cannons and 6 concussion missiles

Maneuverabiltity: High

Armor and Sheilds: Low
