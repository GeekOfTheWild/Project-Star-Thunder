Game Modes

There are many game modes. Which will you Choose?

Note:

In all game modes upon your vehicle being destroyed you lose an amount of resources depending on the vehicle.

Planetary Assault

Length: 30-60 minutes

Singleplayer: No

Multiplayer: Yes

Vehicles: All

Number of players: 50 per team plus cpus

In Project Star Thunders flagship game mode (no pun intended), PA (Planetary Assault), there are two different factions in play, one who defends and one who attacks a base on a planet across six different maps. The objective for the attacking team is to destroy the enemy base. The attacking faction wins if they do this in under an hour and before all the attacking teams capital ships and flagship are destroyed. Otherwise the defenders win. If you are the attacking team you can spawn as a starship or gunship from capital ships and your flagship or any type of vehicle from various outpost etc. around the map, but all of these start on the defenders team and you must claim them for you're team to use them. As the defending team you can spawn from your base or from the aforementioned outposts as long as they are still on your side.

Dogfights

Length: 5-15 minutes

Singleplayer: Yes

Multiplayer: Yes

Vehicles: Starships and Gunships

Number of players: 15 per team

In this game mode there are two factions who have to battle it out and try to get to 50 kills before each other.

Battle Royale: Starships

Length: 5-10 minutes

Singleplayer: No

Multiplayer: Yes

Vehicles: Starships and Gunships

Number of players: 30

Players per team: 1,2,3,5

This is the standard battle royale where the last one flying wins and you don't get any respawns, although a teammate can revive you within 60 seconds of you being destroyed.

Battle Royale: Ground and air

Length: 5-10 minutes

Singleplayer: No

Multiplayer: Yes

Vehicles: Ground, air and Gunships

Number of players: 30

Players per team: 1,2,3,5

This is the standard battle royale where the last one left alive wins and you don't get any respawns, although a teammate can revive you within 60 seconds of you being destroyed.

The Campaign

Length: Multiple hours depending on skill level

Singleplayer: Yes

Multiplayer: No

Vehicles: All

Number of players: 1 plus cpus

In the Campaign you play through a series of canon major battles as set vehicles starting from The Battle of Naboo and ending with The Battle of Exogol. You must complete every battle in an era in both available factions in order to progress to the next era (e.g. you need to do The Battle of Kashyk with both the Republic and the Seperatists in order to get to the Original Trilogy era). This game mode allows you to try out different vehicles whithout you having to unlock them aswell as get some pretty nice rewards

Bounty Hunters

Length: You can play for as long as you like because the bountys rotate periodically

Singleplayer: Yes

Multiplayer: Yes (with co-op to do harder bountys)

Vehicles: All with a unique feature to leave your vehicle and store vehicles in other vehicles

Number of players: 1-3

In Bounty Hunters you play as a Mandalorian with random bountys. These bountys are based on your current level, meaning that as you get further into the game you the bountys you have to do also get gradually harder. These bountys are constantly changing, meaning that you can play for hundreds of hours without getting bored. In this game mode instead of there being a tech tree you can buy vehicles from an assortment of manufacturers, many-if not all-of which are not accesible in the main game
